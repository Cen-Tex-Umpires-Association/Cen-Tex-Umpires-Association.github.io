# CenTex Umpire Association

**TASO Baseball Central Texas Chapter**

We are a non-profit chapter of high school baseball officials proudly serving the Central Texas area. Our members officiate UIL and TAPPS baseball games across Austin, Cedar Park, Georgetown, Round Rock, Liberty Hill, Marble Falls, Manor, Elgin, and surrounding communities.

## Our Mission

- Providing trained and professional baseball officials for high school games
- Continuous education and development of our members
- Upholding the highest standards of officiating in Texas high school baseball

## Get Started

1. Register with [TASO](https://taso.org/) and select the Central Texas chapter
2. Complete required training and background check
3. Attend chapter meetings and training sessions
4. Start receiving game assignments

## Affiliation

We are affiliated with the [Texas Association of Sports Officials (TASO)](https://taso.org/) and operate under [UIL](https://www.uiltexas.org/) guidelines for high school baseball officiating.
