CenTex conducted its Fall Training clinic in Elgin on Friday, December 5th and Saturday, December 6th, 2025.

## Friday, December 5th

- **Time:** 7:00 PM (promptly)
- **Location:** Meeting room
- **Dress:** Business casual

## Saturday, December 6th

- **Time:** 9:00 AM (promptly)
- **Location:** All-purpose building
- **Dress:** Base uniform with black shirt
- **Bring:** Your gear

We had live pitching and focused on timing and judgment.

## What was Provided

- Lunch on Saturday
- All drinks

Big thanks to our Training Coordinator Gordon Glaze for organizing the training, and to Elgin High School for providing the venue and facilities access.
