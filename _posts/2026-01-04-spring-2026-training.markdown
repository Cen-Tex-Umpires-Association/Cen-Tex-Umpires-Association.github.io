Join us for our Spring 2026 Training clinic at Vista Ridge High School!

Scrimmages start January 31st, so this training will help everyone prepare for the season.

**Location:** [Vista Ridge High School](https://maps.google.com/?q=200+S+Vista+Ridge+Blvd,+Cedar+Park,+TX+78613){:target="_blank" rel="noopener"}

## Friday, January 16th

- **Time:** 7:00 PM
- **Focus:** Classroom instruction
- **Where:** Enter through the classroom entrance (see campus map below)

## Saturday, January 17th

- **Time:** 10:00 AM
- **Focus:** On-field training at the baseball field

## Sunday, January 18th (If needed)

- **Time:** 9:00 AM
- **Focus:** On-field training continues (half day, depending on Saturday progress)
