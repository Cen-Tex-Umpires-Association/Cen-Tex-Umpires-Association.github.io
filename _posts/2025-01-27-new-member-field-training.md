The TASO Cen-Tex Baseball Chapter is hosting a **New Member Field Training** session.

## Details

- **Date:** Sunday, February 2, 2025
- **Time:** 9:00 AM to approximately 3:00 PM (no lunch break)
- **Location:** Leander High School Baseball Field ([Get Directions](https://maps.app.goo.gl/9tRjbAgJfsJe64R38))

## What We'll Cover

We will cover working the 2-man umpire system in detail.

## What to Bring

- Base uniform with black shirt
- Plate mask

We look forward to seeing our new members on the field!
